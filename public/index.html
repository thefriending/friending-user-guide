2 Introduction
2.1 Product overview
is an online dating, friendship, and social networking mobile application that
features user-created questionnaires and multiple choice questions. has two primary
features: joining groups to nd people similar to you or registering for events happening in
your local area.
You can create a group around one of your interests, then dene questionnaires that
can be used to match other members of the group. You can join these groups and ll in
questionnaires to be matched with members of the group. The questionnaire answers are
used to determine a mutual match based on the your and others responses. You will receive a
notication if a mutual match is found.
Events are managed by an event host that controls the event. Each event has a questionnaire
that you will ll out to join. You can invite people to join your event, and when you are
ready start the processing of pairing up people in the event. The participants of your party
will then be matched into pairs. You will receive a notication when a match is set.
